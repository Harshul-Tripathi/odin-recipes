Cheesy Chicken Quesadilla


Loaded with melted cheese and tender chicken, this quesadilla is the perfect mix of comfort and crunch—quick.

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked, shredded chicken
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 1/2 onion and bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 tsp taco seasoning
  • 1 tsp oil or butter
  • Salsa or sour cream (to serve)
  1. Heat oil, sauté onion and bell pepper until soft
  2. Add chicken and taco seasoning, cook for 2-3 mins
  3. Place tortilla in pan, layer cheese and chicken mix on one half
  4. Fold and cook both sides until golden and cheese melts
  5. Slice into wedges
  6. Serve hot with salsa or sour cream
