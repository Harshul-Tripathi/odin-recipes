Nigiri Sushi


Delicate and flavorful, Nigiri sushi showcases the pure essence of fresh fish over seasoned rice.

  • 1 cup sushi rice
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Fresh raw fish (salmon, tuna, or shrimp - sliced)
  • Wasabi paste
  • Soy sauce (to serve)
  1. Cook rice, mix with vinegar, sugar, and salt
  2. Let cool
  3. Wet hands, shape small rice ovals (bite-sized)
  4. Add a dab of wasabi on each rice oval
  5. Place a slice of fish over each and press gently
  6. Serve with soy sauce on the side
